Stay with Us

Pembrook Castle offers a wide selection of holiday accommodation for couples, families and groups wishing to explore beautiful Highland Perthshire. Choose from our Highland Lodges, cottages and luxurious Shepherd Huts, which span the estate. Within the grounds of Pembrook Castle, we offer a range of Woodland Lodges, as well a range of options at our comfortable Caravan Park.


Pembrook Castle & Gardens

Two luxury bespoke Shepherd Huts situated in Tulliemet valley beneath ancient oaks on an old shepherd's hill track, our huts enjoy breathtakingly long views across Strathtay and beyond. Our huts are luxurious. Decorated beautifully with block printed linens, hand-stitched quilts, and the comfiest king-size bedchamber. Our Woodland Lodges provide the perfect getaway for those looking to stay in the heart of nature, whilst providing you with all the comforts of home.
